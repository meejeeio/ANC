OGTT Analysis (75g — GDM Diagnosis per IADPSG/WHO 2013)

Enter values in mg/dL. GDM is diagnosed if any one value meets or exceeds the threshold:
Fasting ≥ 92  ·  1-hour ≥ 180  ·  2-hour ≥ 153
Conversion: mg/dL = mmol/L × 18.018  |  mmol/L = mg/dL ÷ 18.018
OGTT criteria: IADPSG 2010 / WHO 2013 (75g oral glucose, venous plasma). Diagnosis requires ≥1 abnormal value. Always confirm with clinical judgement.
